Senior Mens

The Men's section of the Doncaster Hockey Club is made up of 6 teams ranging from Premier League to Metro that accommodate players of all skill levels and expectations. The excellent junior development combined with support from senior players offers a supportive and inclusive environment. Excellent coaching, mentoring and administration is crucial to help grow and develop the club as a whole. We have sides for all abilities and are proud of each and every one of our players. We welcome players returning to the sport or those having a go for the first time. There are opportunities to play or support and coach the teams. We have a strong coaching support for all sides we look forward to an inclusive and bright future.

  • Metro A

Midweek Ladies

Doncaster Hockey Club offers informal social Midweek Ladies Hockey program. New participants are warmly welcomed and all skills and fitness levels are catered for. Current participants are a mixture of “stay at home mums”, some of whom have only recently been introduced to hockey through their children playing in our Juniors. Retired womens players, new time mums, ladies who haven’t played since school days, uni students and part time workers are just some of our group. The aim is to be flexible around work, family and life, so you don’t have to attend every week. Hockey sticks and shin pads are available to borrow if required. Free coaching and assistance is also available during the sessions.

Times

  • The midweek ladies program is conducted on a Wednesday 9.15am – 10.45am ( excluding school holidays).

Masters / Midweek

Masters and midweek teams of the Doncaster Hockey Club are a very valuable and respected element, which boasts players, committee members, coaches, and other worthy players, who consider their continued participation an important part of their lives without committing to weekend sport.
